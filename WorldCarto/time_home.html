Time, Space, and Travel

A cartogram is a map in which some thematic mapping variable is substituted for land area or distance. The geometry or space of the map is distorted, sometimes extremely, in order to convey the information of this alternate variable. The variable substituted in this map is visits to different countries by 4 people over the span of ten years. The cartogram was created using cartogram software ScapeToad and animated using D3.

  • Time: 10 years
  • Space: over 378,580 miles
  • Travel: 63 countries

Authors: Nina Kerr, Rachel Saunders, Alicia De Jong, and Greg Mireles.
Design: HTML5 UP.
